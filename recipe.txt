Shakshouka

1 can of crushed tomatoes
2 cloves garlic
1/2 medium yellow onion
2 tbs olive oil
4 eggs
1 tbs cumin

Sautee the onion and garlic in olive oil at medium-high heat. 
Add cumin and stir until elements are coated.
Add crushed tomatoes and allow to boil. Lower heat and simmer 20 minutes. Add salt to taste. 
Use a spoon to create divots for eggs in the mixture. Crack eggs one by one into a ramakin and transfer gently into divots. 
Cover and allow to cook for 10 minutes. 
Optional: 
Garnish with parsely and add crumbled feta. Serve with toasted baguette.
